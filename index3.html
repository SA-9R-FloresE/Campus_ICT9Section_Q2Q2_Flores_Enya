About!

Robotics is developed in schools for the youth, also known as the future of the nation. It is our responsibility to pass the flame to our youth, and robotics is for our technological future and part of our advancements as a society.

This serves as the official page to serve for the club. This club still undergoes school rules, and shall preserve them in club meetings and spaces alike. If you/your kid have an interest, it is preferred if you recommend them this club. :)
